My Dogs

I am simply going to describe my wonderful pets and their wonderful personality!

Nala: The Oldest

Nala is the first dog we got and she is 4 years old! She is a very friendly dog who loves to play and cuddles! She is also incredibly intelligent and knows a lot of tricks!

Luna: The Cat

Luna is the second oldest dog I have. She is by far the weirdest one of the pack. She is very shy and cuddly at the same time. She loves stealing socks and sleeping! My family and I often describe her as a cat!

Chiquis: The Troublemaker

Chiquis is the last and youngest dog that I have. She is very energetic and playful! She is also the most mischievous. She loves ripping up toys and hiding underneath the bed. She's an angel once you get her exhausted from playing or walking!
